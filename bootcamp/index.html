Reading the theory is the first step. Building the muscle memory to deploy and debug trillion-parameter systems is the next. Join Architects, Operators, and Builders for an intensive 5-day program that bridges the physics of scale and your company's infrastructure.

What You Will Do

Each day maps to the book's parts and companion code.

Day 1: The Math of the Cluster

Build a bottom-up TCO model for your next training run, calculate critical batch size, and predict performance using Chinchilla scaling laws.

Day 2: Distributed Diagnostics

Intentionally break an 8-GPU cluster and debug NCCL timeouts and thermal stragglers in real time.

Day 3: The Memory Wall

Write custom kernels and deploy PagedAttention to serve a 70B model with a 10x throughput increase.

Day 4: Agentic Engineering

Map a naive RAG pipeline onto a multi-tier memory architecture with GraphRAG and semantic caching.

Day 5: The GreenOps Mandate

Architect a spatio-temporal scheduler that shifts training jobs based on live carbon grid-intensity data.
